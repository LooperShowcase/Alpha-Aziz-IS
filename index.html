In Brawl Stars, players battle against other players or AI opponents in multiple game modes. Players can choose between different Brawlers, with are divided among 5 rarities (Rare, Super Rare, Epic, Mythic, Legendary). Brawlers can be unlocked through the Starr Road, the Brawl Pass, the Trophy Road, or purchased through the Shop to use in battles. In December 2022, a new progressive unlock system called the "Starr Road" replaced Boxes as the method to unlock Brawlers. The cost to unlock each Brawler on the Starr Road depends on its rarity.
